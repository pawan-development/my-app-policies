MindLog

Privacy Policy · Last updated: June 2025

1. Introduction

MindLog is a personal productivity app for managing todos, grocery lists, notes, and daily tasks. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

2. Data Collection

MindLog does NOT collect any personal data. All information you enter (tasks, lists, notes) is stored locally on your device only using AsyncStorage. No data is transmitted to any server or third party.

3. Internet Access

MindLog does not require internet access and works completely offline. No network requests are made by the app.

4. Data Storage & Security

All your data stays on your device. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data. We do not have access to your data at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

MindLog does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services of any kind.

6. Children's Privacy

MindLog does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The app contains no harmful content.

7. Your Rights

Since we collect no data, there is nothing to request, modify, or delete from our side. You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the new policy.
